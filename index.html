ThreadLuxe

Dress to Impress for Less

From bat mitzvahs, to quinceaneras, to sweet sixteens, ThreadLuxe has always been able to deliver an affordable alternative to spending hundreds of dollars on a one-time wear outfit. ThreadLuxe is a formal attire rental agency, where owners of one-time wear can rent out their used outfits. Clients who cannot afford high-end products but still want to look gorgeous for their special occasion can rent out these lightly used outfits for affordable prices
